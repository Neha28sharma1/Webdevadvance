Explore Jönköping City Districts

Our city center consist of several districts. East Town, West Town, Spira, Match Town, the City Park, Jönköping Grand Station, Jönköping University and the Atoll. Discover our districts below!

Our Districts

  • East Town (Öster) Popular shopping area with cafés and restaurants.
  • West Town (Väster) Historic streets with boutiques and local shops.
  • Spira Cultural center with theatre, concerts and events.
  • Match Town Historic match industry district with museums.
  • City Park Beautiful park with views over Jönköping.
  • Jönköping Grand StationMain travel hub connecting trains and buses.
  • Jönköping University International university campus.
  • Atoll Modern waterfront district with restaurants and nightlife.
